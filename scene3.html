Estimated change in the death rate from non-optimal temperatures from 2030 to 2090

  • Countries such as Nigeria, Bangladesh, and Qatar are expected to see an increase in temperature related deaths with every 0.5°C of temperature increase by 2030 and beyond.
  • In countries like the United Kingdom, Sweden, and Canada, warmer temperatures are projected to reduce temperature-related deaths, likely due to milder winters and improved climate resilience.
  • In Spain, South Africa, and Brazil there is a projected decline in heat-related deaths until mid-century, followed with numbers rising by the end of the century.
