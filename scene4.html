Projected change in temperature-related death rate vs. GDP per capita

  • Climate change exacerbates inequalities, with poorer countries bearing the brunt of increased heat-related mortality, while wealthier nations experience fewer deaths due to better resources and infrastructure.
  • Heat-related death rates are expected to rise in many low-income countries, while most high-income countries in Europe and North America will see a decline in deaths, highlighting disparities in climate resilience.
